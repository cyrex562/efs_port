Prana-bindu

Now, in opening oneself to the Light, one must take care to avoid the enticing snares of a legion of dubious thrones; for in so doing one can easily replicate the folly of the Prana-bindu mystics of the Soma path. These worthies, mistaking clever disfigurement for enlightenment, willingly perform fleshly manipulations such as the Pancreator never intended, and contort themselves into shapes such as scions of Urth were never meant to assume.
