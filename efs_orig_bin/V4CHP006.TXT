Vau Psychology

Can the Vau be comprehended? Most assuredly; indeed, they encourage it, though they admit it not. For to understand the vicissitudes of the Vau is to reject all that is human, to shed one's soul for a numbing yoke. Certes, no scion of Urth who has gone among the Vau has retained the Pancreator's manifesto, but has instead become enraptured with the deceitful race and has returned, if at all, as their merest dupe.
