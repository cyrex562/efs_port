" and so the pilot, having mounted and tamed the craft, turns its nose full unto the target. And then man and mount plummet from the heavens to smite the infidel not with conjured beams and war-rockets, but with the craft itself. Certainly the Pancreator must smile on such heroism, and I aver that the awful explosion of the pilots' demise is in reality the instantaneous transubstantiation of their soul-matter into purest celestial flame."
