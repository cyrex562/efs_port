And when we speak of renegades, let us not forget the company of the Ur-Ukar mercenary Baal oj Ak; nay, let us save our most potent maledictions for this one, whose crimes are legion; who has distilled the very plague-spirits of the Ukari Gorges and launched them over the walls of Harthane City, so that all in its bounds, every man, woman and child, fell gasping and reeking of plague and leaking of pus.
